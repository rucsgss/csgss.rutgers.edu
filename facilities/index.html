CS Kitchen

The third floor lobby of the CoRE building contains a kitchen for use by all DCS faculty, staff, and students. The kitchen is run by members of CSGSS on a volunteer basis. If you have any questions or suggestions, or if you'd like to help out, please contact the current CSGSS president.

Graduate Student Lounge (Hill 350)

The GSL is a space where graduate students can go to relax, watch a movie, or sit and talk with friends. It also contains mailboxes for TAs, PTLs, and postdocs.
